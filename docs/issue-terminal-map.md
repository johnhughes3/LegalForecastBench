## Route vocabulary

- **Implement** means an existing Bead owns unfinished acceptance work.
- **Evidence-close** means current code, merged PRs, and artifacts must be checked before closing an issue whose dated recipe may be stale.
- **Focused successor** means the broad issue stays linked to a narrower Bead with explicit acceptance criteria.
- **External blocker** means reactivation requires named evidence or authority outside the repository.
- **Dated defer** means the item remains open until a named milestone or condition, not merely until someone revisits it.

Issue comments and the passive `.beads/issues.jsonl` export are supporting history only; no terminal route below relies on either as its sole authority.

## Refresh and audit procedure

The map is complete only when its machine-readable `live-open-issues` manifest exactly matches the current open GitHub issue numbers and every row still names a terminal route, a milestone or exact reactivation condition, an owner lane, and verifiable closure evidence.
